Biryani

Description

Vegetarian Biryani is a fragrant, flavorful rice dish made with a medley of vegetables, spices, and herbs. Popular across South Asia, it is a hearty and satisfying meal that doesn‚Äôt compromise on taste. Unlike meat biryanis, the vegetarian version emphasizes the freshness of the produce and the aromatic richness of the spices.

To make a delicious vegetarian biryani, you'll need the following ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, potatoes)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala or whole spices (cardamom, cloves, bay leaves, cinnamon)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk (optional)
  • Salt and oil
The preparation involves the following steps:
  1. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook it until 80% done and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes, yogurt, and biryani spices. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Mix in chopped vegetables and cook until slightly tender.
  5. In a large pot, layer rice and the cooked vegetable mixture alternately. Top with saffron milk and herbs.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 15-20 minutes until flavors meld together.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.
